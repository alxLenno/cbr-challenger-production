Bible Study Methods

The three devotional and character study methods used throughout the CBR programme.

✍️

F.I.D — Daily Devotional Journal (Cards 1–3)

Write brief FID journal notes each day after your CBR reading. Earn 1 Meditation point per week when done daily.

F Focus — Write the key verse or subject reference from your reading today.
I Insight — Record the key spiritual insight or observation the Holy Spirit revealed to you.
D Doing — Describe the specific practical action or obedience conviction you are prompted to do today.

📌 Share your FID with your PE group at least once per week to earn the 1 Accountability point.

📖

O.P.E.N — Bible Study Method (Cards 4–7)

A deeper 4-step method replacing FID from Card 4 onwards. Study Scripture systematically using these steps:

O Observations — Gather accurate biblical information: facts, names, events, principles, parables, and intriguing details. What does this signify?
P Principles — Discover godly principles by: looking for direct statements of blessing/consequence; investigating why God blessed or cursed individuals; comparing Scripture with Scripture.
E Experience — Reflect prayerfully on the facts and principles to discern what God is saying about your current circumstances.
N Need — Apply the truth or conviction God has impressed on you. Write down what God has prompted you to do today in response to this study (Jos 1:8; John 13:17; James 1:25).
🔍

P.E.R.S.O.N.S — Character Quality Study (Cards 4–7)

A 7-step method to examine and transform character qualities through Scripture. Study at least 5 of the 9 suggested qualities.

P Personal perspective — Self-assessment: what does this quality mean to you and what do others say about it?
E English meaning — Look up the definition of this quality in a dictionary or online.
R References from the Bible — Study provided Scriptures carefully to gain a spiritual understanding of the quality.
S Satan's opposing qualities — Identify negative attributes that counteract the quality you are examining.
O Obedience — Describe practical ways this trait can be demonstrated in the life of a Christian.
N Note — Identify ways to apply insights about this quality specifically to maintain CBR discipline for life.
S Stirring others — Which Bible reading friends would you testify to about this quality to help them keep CBR?

9 Suggested Character Qualities to Study:

  • Accountability
  • Commitment
  • Diligence
  • Faithfulness
  • Honesty
  • New Self
  • Perseverance
  • Reliability
  • Watchfulness
