Bible Study Methods

The three devotional and character study methods used throughout the CBR programme.

F.I.D — Daily Devotional Journal (Cards 1–3)

Write brief FID journal notes each day after your CBR reading. Earn 1 Meditation point per week when done daily.

F

Focus — Write the key verse or subject reference from your reading today.

I

Insight — Record the key spiritual insight or observation the Holy Spirit revealed to you.

D

Doing — Describe the specific practical action or obedience conviction you are prompted to do today.

Share your FID with your PE group at least once per week to earn the 1 Accountability point.

O.P.E.N — Bible Study Method (Cards 4–7)

A deeper 4-step method replacing FID from Card 4 onwards. Study Scripture systematically using these steps:

O

Observations — Gather accurate biblical information: facts, names, events, principles, parables, and intriguing details.

P

Principles — Discover godly principles by investigating why God blessed or cursed individuals.

E

Experience — Reflect prayerfully on the facts and principles to discern what God is saying about your current circumstances.

N

Need — Apply the truth or conviction God has impressed on you. Write down what God has prompted you to do.

P.E.R.S.O.N.S — Character Study (Cards 4–7)

A 7-step method to examine and transform character qualities through Scripture. Study at least 5 of the 9 suggested qualities.

P

Personal perspective — Self-assessment: what does this quality mean to you?

E

English meaning — Look up the definition of this quality.

R

References — Study provided Scriptures to gain a spiritual understanding.

S

Satan's opposing qualities — Identify negative attributes that counteract this quality.

O

Obedience — Practical ways this trait can be demonstrated in life.

N

Note — How to apply insights to maintain CBR discipline for life.

S

Stirring others — Which friends would you testify to about this quality?

9 Suggested Character Qualities to Study:

  • Accountability
  • Commitment
  • Diligence
  • Faithfulness
  • Honesty
  • New Self
  • Perseverance
  • Reliability
  • Watchfulness
